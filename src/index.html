Our Garden

Welcome to the most trusted Flower Boutque in Deutschland. Types of Flowers: Consider planting a variety of flowers that are popular in floral arrangements. Some popular choices include roses, tulips, daisies, lilies, sunflowers, and orchids. Seasonal Varieties: Plan your garden to have flowers that bloom in different seasons. This will ensure you have a consistent supply of fresh flowers year-round. Color Palette: Choose a color palette that aligns with your shop's branding or the current trends. Coordinated colors can create an appealing visual display. Arrangement Styles: Design your garden to reflect various arrangement styles, such as bouquets, centerpieces, and wreaths. This will allow customers to see how different flowers can be combined. Care Instructions: Display care instructions for each type of flower. This could include information on watering, sunlight, pruning, and fertilizing. Landscaping: Pay attention to the layout of your garden. Create pathways for customers to walk through and appreciate the flowers. Use borders and edging to define different flowerbeds. Fragrance: Incorporate fragrant flowers into your garden. Fragrance adds an extra sensory dimension and can be very appealing to customers.
